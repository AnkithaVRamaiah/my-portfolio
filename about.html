About Me

Hi, I’m Ankitha V — a passionate tech professional transitioning from software development to DevOps and cloud engineering.

Since then, I’ve worked on:

  • Deploying apps to Kubernetes clusters
  • Provisioning AWS infrastructure with Terraform

I’m now actively seeking DevOps or Cloud Engineering opportunities where I can apply my skills, grow further, and contribute to exciting projects in a collaborative environment.
