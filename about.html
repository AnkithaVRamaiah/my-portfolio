About Me

Hi, I'm Ankitha V.

I started my career in August 2022 as an Associate Software Engineer, mainly working on Java-based applications. I was involved in the full development cycle — writing code, fixing bugs, and collaborating with teams using tools like GitLab and Jira. While I enjoyed backend development, I found myself increasingly curious about what happens after the code is written — how it’s built, tested, deployed, and managed.

That curiosity led me to DevOps.

Even during my developer role, I got a chance to work closely with the DevOps team — writing and reviewing Dockerfiles, contributing to Jenkins pipelines, and helping with deployments to dev and QA environments. I wasn’t officially in a DevOps role, but I knew it was something I wanted to explore further.

In March 2024, I stepped away from my job for personal reasons. Since then, I’ve fully focused on building my DevOps skills. I’ve spent my time learning through real hands-on projects — not just tutorials. I’ve worked on:

  • Containerizing applications using Docker
  • Creating CI/CD pipelines with Jenkins and GitHub Actions
  • Deploying apps to Kubernetes clusters (Minikube & EKS)
  • Managing Helm charts for Kubernetes deployments
  • Writing Terraform scripts to automate AWS infrastructure
  • Using Ansible for configuration management
  • Practicing monitoring with CloudWatch, Prometheus, and Grafana

Now, I’m actively looking for DevOps or Cloud Engineering opportunities where I can apply what I’ve learned, contribute to real-world projects, and keep growing in a collaborative team.

Thanks for taking the time to get to know me!
